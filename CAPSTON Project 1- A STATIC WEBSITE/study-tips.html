Effective Study Tips

  • Create a dedicated study space free from distractions.
  • Use the Pomodoro Technique: study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break.
  • Practice active recall by testing yourself on the material.
  • Teach what you've learned to someone else to reinforce your understanding.
  • Stay organized with a study schedule and set specific goals for each session.
  • Use diagrams and mind maps
  • Take regular breaks to avoid burnout.
